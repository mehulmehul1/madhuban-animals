🐎 Quadruped Movement Fix: Diagonal vs Perpendicular

❌ BEFORE (Perpendicular Movement)

Mouse: 🎯
⬆️ Step 1: Move vertically
🐎
➡️ Step 2: Move horizontally
🐎────🎯

Problem: Hard assignment to ground level

foot.target.y = this.groundLevel;

Result: Two-step perpendicular movement pattern

✅ AFTER (Direct Diagonal Movement)

Mouse: 🎯
↗️ Direct diagonal movement
🐎╱
    ╱
   🎯

Fix: Smooth interpolation like crane

foot.target.x += (targetX - foot.target.x) * 0.15;
foot.target.y += (targetY - foot.target.y) * 0.15;

Result: Direct diagonal movement like crane! 🎉

  1. Open your application (index.html)
  2. Press 3 for Horse or 4 for Lizard
  3. Click anywhere on canvas - watch the movement pattern

🔧 Technical Changes Made

Key Fix: Changed from hard assignment to smooth interpolation

Movement Speed: 0.15 (slightly faster than crane's 0.1 for responsiveness)

Benefits:

  • ✅ Direct diagonal movement like crane
  • ✅ Maintains realistic gait animations
  • ✅ Smooth transitions without jarring jumps
  • ✅ Works with adaptive ground system
