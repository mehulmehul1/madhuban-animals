🔧 Fix #1: Anatomical Foot Spacing

❌ BEFORE (Problem)

Tiny arbitrary offsets:

frontBackOffset = isFront ? 15 : -15

Result: Front and hind feet too close together, looked unnatural

✅ AFTER (Fixed)

Real anatomical proportions:

shoulderHipOffset = shoulderHipDistance / 2

Result: Proper spacing based on creature anatomy

Horse Proportions:
Shoulder-to-Hip: 120 units | Leg Spacing: 45 units

Lizard Proportions:
Shoulder-to-Hip: 80 units | Leg Spacing: 35 units

🔄 Fix #2: Anti-Spinning Body Movement

❌ BEFORE (Problem)

Unlimited angular velocity:

bodyHeading += walkTurnBlend * angleDiff

Result: Body would spin rapidly on abrupt direction changes

✅ AFTER (Fixed)

Limited turn rate:

limitedAngleDiff = Math.min(|angleDiff|, maxTurnRate)

Result: Smooth, gradual turns like real animals

Turn rate limited to: maxTurnRate = 0.04 radians per frame
